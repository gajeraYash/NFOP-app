Welcome to the
Newark Fraternal Order of Police

The Newark Fraternal Order of Police Lodge#12 is an active Union Office that represents about 850 active police officers and over 700 retirees. We protect our officers rights in incidents where an officer has taken a legal action while on or off duty. We negociate for their benefits, salaries and work schedules. Along with just being an active lodge. We also have great support form within our communities that become associate members and show their support for our law enforcement community. With their support we donate to various organizations.
